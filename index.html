Powwow Is an all day live and local music event in the southern Indiana area. We have music and games all day with camping at night.

STORY

Powwow is all about live and local music from and for the southern Indiana area. Our mission is to make sure our campers have the greatest time that we can supply. Mostly local bands supply live music all day for attendees to listen to and watch. Various games and activities will be set up throughout the grounds.

Camping

We want people to be safe, and encourage anyone that wants to camp to crash and stay the night with us.
